Flutterwave For Businness: The Great journey of F4B

What do you know about F4B?

Flutterwave for bussiness also known as F4B is a service sprcifiaclly built for merchants to cater for their day to day needs ranging from getting payment links, receiving payments from customers and also facilitate processing of such payments a very low cost

It also come with a tons of advantages over other procesing apps from other companies.

Flutterwave has also been noted to be the face behind some other services created to cater for moving and transfering of funds, hence taking a giant stride to solve the problem currently affecting both locally and globally disbursement of money in, into and out of Africa

Few of the solutions already provided to the payment space by Flutterware are listed below:

  1. Send By Flutterwave: This is a remittance app that facilitates movement of money Globally. The service is currently available in all African countries, US, Uk and Europe and MENA
  2. Flutterwave for Business: As stated earlier, this app cater for payouts in some African countries. It is curremtly available in Anglophone west African Countries like Ghana and Nigeria, Francophone African countries like Cameroon, Zambia, Senegal etc., East African Countries like Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Tanzania and Ethiopia.
  3. Money Wave: This integration caters for POS and terminal partnerships with banks and fintechs,
  4. Tuition: This App caters for payment of school fees home and abroad. The integration was first lauched in partnerships with over 40 universities in UK. This allows international students to make payment without stress
